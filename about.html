About Me

I'm a 15-year old geek living in Falls Church, Virginia. I love photography, hiking, writing, cycling, music, and tech. I go to George Mason High School, where I'm currently in ninth grade. I'm a Zen Buddhist.

In my free time I do photography, design websites, or play guitar. I also enjoy biking, backpacking, climbing (rock or otherwise), and writing. I love listening to nearly all genres of music, including rock, alternative, electronic, pop, etc. If you like, you can listen to a playlist of all my favorite music here. I'm part of the Technology Student Association and FIRST Robotics.

What I've done

Throughout my life I've been interested in technology, and have gained experience in many areas of it, including CAD, sound, video, TV broadcast directing, game design, app development, robotics, back-end, photography & graphic design, writing/blogging & electronics and hardware, web, and more.

I've volunteered twice at New Day, a Chinese foster home for orphans with special needs. I've also been to Malaysia and lived in Chicago for four years. In 8th grade I achieved the Gold award in Technology Student Association, and also directed my school's Morning Announcements. I competed in the FIRST Lego Robotics challenge four times during Middle School. I played Trumpet for four years before switching to Guitar.

What I'm going to do

After finishing high school at George Mason I'm going to apply to Stanford, MIT, or the University of Michigan. Then I want to work at Google, or a web agency such as Viget or Interactive Strategies. Later in life I plan to be a tech teacher and bring new teaching methods to elementary/Middle School Computer Science education. That is, assuming human teachers are not yet obsolete.
